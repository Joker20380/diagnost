{% trans "Our Story" %}

{% blocktrans %} AI-Diagnostics was founded in Ghent, Belgium, by professionals with hands-on experience at the very bottom of the automotive service chain — where uncertainty, misdiagnosis, and lack of transparency are most visible. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Years of practical work in vehicle diagnostics revealed a systemic problem: modern cars generate vast amounts of data, yet many service decisions are still made without structured analysis or clear reasoning. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} The company was built to change this paradigm. Instead of selling “diagnostics” as a procedure, we focus on reducing uncertainty — for technicians, service managers, and vehicle owners alike. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Based in Ghent, at the heart of Europe’s technological and automotive ecosystem, AI-Diagnostics combines real diagnostic experience with software engineering, artificial intelligence, and strict European standards for quality, safety, and accountability. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Today, AI-Diagnostics is an evolving platform and service focused on: {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} • Intelligent fault interpretation.
• Risk-aware diagnostic decision support.
• High-quality diagnostic documentation.
• Responsible use of AI in technical systems. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} The core principle remains unchanged: respect for engineering, respect for data, and respect for every vehicle and client. {% endblocktrans %}
